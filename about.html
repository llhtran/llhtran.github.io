Lien Tran

Vietnamese: Tran Le Hong Lien


I was born in Russia to Vietnamese parents. Growing up, I lived in Moscow, Saigon, Hanoi and Stockholm before coming to the US for college at Yale University.

I currently work at Asana as a software engineer. I also pursue a multitude of personal and collaborative projects that involve music, visual arts and software development. I am interested in the intersection of arts and technology, and how it could be used to influence human behavior and global culture.
